The 3rd African Workshop on Genome Editing

CRISPR-Cas9 & AI for Genome Editing

2-4 June, Sousse, Tunisia

The African Workshop on CRISPR & Gene Editing, hosted by MVAI Startup and the Pasteur Institute of Tunis, is a groundbreaking event in genomic innovation. Led by experts, this conference explores cutting-edge technologies in genome editing and CRISPR, offering invaluable insights and networking opportunities.

Experience an immersive journey into precision gene editing technology at MVAI's workshop. Expand your horizons and join us for this exciting learning opportunity. We look forward to welcoming you!
